My top 3 movies of all-time.

Monsters. Inc

This is my favorite movie. It has lots of cool looking monsters and the journey is funny and thrilling.

Cars

This is a fun movie. It has cool looking talking cars and the thrill of racing.

Captain America: The Winter Soldier

It is about the hero Captain America trying to find his friend. He goes on a journey which is action-packed and has some horror.
